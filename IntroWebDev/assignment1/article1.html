The Rise of Cybersecurity Threats in 2026

Just as the new year is rung in, these are the rising cybersecurity threats to you and your organization.

Key Cybersecurity Threats in 2026

  1. Supply Chain Attacks: Attackers are increasingly targeting the third-party vendors and suppliers of larger organizations to compormise systems. See the recent 2/2 supply chain attack of notepad++
  2. AI-Powered Attacks: Anyone with internet access can now hack your computer with AI. Even from a chromebook!.
  3. Ransomware Evolution: Ransomware attacks are still around and believe it or not, bitcoin is still around! Protect your files and your network.

For more information on cybersecurity best practices, visit the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA).
